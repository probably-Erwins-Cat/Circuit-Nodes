# Circuit Nodes Puzzle Pieces

All Circuit Nodes puzzle pieces are collected and constantly updated here. Each piece represents a specific circuit component or connection type, named according to their schematic symbols rather than specific component numbers. For example, the folder for an n-channel MOSFET will contain compatible part numbers like the 2N7000.

## Design Standard

All puzzle pieces follow a consistent design template to ensure they work together seamlessly:

- **Physical dimensions**: 40×40 mm square outline with 4 mm radius corners
- **Mounting**: Four M3-compatible screw holes in the corners (2.9–3.0 mm diameter, 4 mm from edges)
- **Visual style**: 2 mm thick pictogram lines with rounded edges where possible
- **Electrical layout**: Copper pour kept at least 2 mm from board edges and not connected to GND or power supply
- **Connector interface**: Integrated magnetic pogo connectors (see the dedicated pogo-connector folder)

## Board-to-Board Connectors

All pieces are designed for integrated magnetic pogo connectors. The layout and spacing of each puzzle piece are carefully matched to these connectors for reliable, automatic alignment when pieces are joined.
